OneSchool

OneSchool helps you stay connected with classmates by automatically adding you to the Discord group chats for the courses you're enrolled in.

3 Easy Steps

Information

OneSchool uses the classes you're taking and automatically places you in the corresponding group chats for those classes. Information provided to OneSchool may be used for security purposes and to make other Mark Zakharyan Productions more useful to you.

Step One

Sign in with school Google account

This is required to ensure security by preventing fake or unathorized accounts from accessing your class chats.

Step Two

Sign in with Discord

This will allow OneSchool to automatically add you to the proper groupchats. Discord is also used to ensure security.

Step Three

Enter your classes

Entering the classes you're taking allows OneSchool to place you in the corresponding chats for your classes.
